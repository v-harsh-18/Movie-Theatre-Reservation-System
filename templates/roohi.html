  • Power Packed one time action entertainer🔥🔥🔥🔥 24.08.2018, 17:53 by Maniratnn Parakh 9.0

    Mumbai Saga Based on true events this is a thunderous ,high octane mass action film,John Abraham portrays Amartya Rao fearlessly with his Swag , Mahesh Manjrekar as Bhau is rock solid in the dark avatar and Emraan Hashmi steals the show in his cop avatar and puts up a dhamakedar show all together . The film has adrenaline pumping action, powerful dialouges and stylish sequences to laud with whistles and talis in the cinemas. The film is crisply edited and has great transition and flow, it puts light on the dark gangster era and how a common family man turns a ruthless gangster . Super Solid BGM adds to the rocking sequences in the film. The Cop Gangster face off between John & Emraan is something to look out for , they both excel big time ,production design is top notch , Bombay in its 80s is well designed and presented, the sets looks realistic except for the extensive use of yellow color filter. What falls short for the film is that the story of the film beautifully depicts an era but fails to dig deep into story of amartya & his close right hand gangsters & specially the ending/climax which looks very abrupt and could have had a better ending, the climax leaves you with a incomplete feeling . Overall this film has flaws in its story line but is definelty a one time paisa vasool entertainer . Watch it for your dose of adrenaline high dose entertainement ,action & masala .
