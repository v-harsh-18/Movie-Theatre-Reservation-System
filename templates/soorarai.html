Easily brings in the emotional attachment from the very beginning, gets us highly invested and feel for the characters. Tears and goosebumps guaranteed in this film. Coming to the performances, Suriya look outstanding in each and every frame. For me, this has to be one of the best performances I have seen after a long time. Actor Suriya has literally lived the character 'Nedumaaran' and in most of the scenes his eyes talk a lot. Don't miss it guys. His performance will leave a huge impact, especially the scene where he needs to fly home to see his sick father. I literally cried out watching that scene. Really heart breaking scene. It reminds me of Suriya's acting in Vaaranam Ayiram when his girlfriend dead. OMG. To be honest his acting skill is more better in Soorarai Pottru. That is wat Suriya is. He always make him better every time. That hardwork and dedication really paid off in this film. Feel really really happy for Suriya. Not forgetting the female lead Aparna, did play a very good role in Suriya's life in the film. One of a memorable character done by and projected really well. Your this character really create impact in every boy's life to get this kind of girl in their life as well. Also in girl's heart to have that caring and understanding feeling in them. Your chemistry with Suriya is incomparable. Another memorable character would be Suriya's mother in this film, Urvashi. She also played very well and bring tears in almost every scene she's in. So touching. All the supporting casts are apt for their role and given their best. Special mentions for Karunas and Mohan Babu. I'm not lying this is the most best Tamil movie I've watched more than 3 times. The feeling that I have when I watched for the first time is still there even I watched it again and again. Suriya's acting is the highlight of Soorarai Pottru. He just nailed it. I loved the storyline so much. Huge applause to Sudha Kongara for this master piece. Hats off to Suriya, Aparna, Urvashi, Karunas, Mohan Babu and all the other cast in the film. Superb performance performed by all of you. Music and bgm given by GV Prakash is superb and it definitely gives more value for the movie. Cinematography and editing is handled well. Soorarai Pottru is a movie that stays really close to our heart and is a big-time inspiration also motivation to all those who believe in themselves to work hard and to never forget their roots. Don't miss this one. . Suriya really proved the word "no pain no gain". Behind every success there is hundreds of sacrifices. Just stay strong and believe in yourself.
